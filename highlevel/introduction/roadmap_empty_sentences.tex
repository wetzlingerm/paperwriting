
\guideline[g:introduction:roadmap_empty_sentences]
    {Avoid empty sentences when outlining the structure.}

\goodbadexample[{\cite[Sec.~I.B.]{Wetzlinger2023TAC}}]{
    Section VII \highlightpart{contains} numerical examples.
}{
    Section VII \highlightpart{demonstrates the performance of the proposed algorithms} on a variety of numerical examples.
}

\noindent
Ideally, the outline of the structure also contains the briefest of summaries of each section.
A sentence that solely repeats the section title does not add any value to the reader and should therefore be avoided.
Of course, this guideline is only applicable if the structure is a separate paragraph or subsection.
