
\guideline[g:wording:explicit_references]
    {Use explicit destinations when referring to other sections.}

\goodbadexample[{\cite[Sec.~V.]{Wetzlinger2024CSL}}]{
    This provides empirical validation for the low polynomial time complexity derived \highlightpart{above}.
}{
    This provides empirical validation for the low polynomial time complexity derived \highlightpart{at the end of Section IV}.
}

\noindent
Clearly specifying the intended section or structural element assists the reader in forming an internal map of how different parts of the paper are interconnected;
the narrower the scope of the reference, the better.
