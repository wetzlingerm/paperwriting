
\guideline[g:references:no_nonpeerreviewed]
    {Avoid citing non-peer-reviewed work.}

\noindent
Non-peer-reviewed work has not undergone formal review to ensure the correctness of its content and is thus more likely to contain errors.
This is especially important when the citation supports key claims or derivations, but less critical when citing numerical examples or content that can be independently verified.
